Ignite Megastars™

Ignite Megastars is a platform setup by C&CD Consulting Limited to discover, develop and promote young talents in sports, via its reality television show series in diverse sporting fields.

Male and Female athletes within the ages of 15 and 18 will have the opportunity to showcase their talent and rise to stardom through a transparent system of voting from Judges, Coaches and Viewers.
