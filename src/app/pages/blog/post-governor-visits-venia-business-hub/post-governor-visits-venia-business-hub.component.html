Lagos state Governor Visits Venia Business Hub

Sports / June, 2021

The Lagos state Governor, Mr.Babajide Sanwolu, visited Venia Business Hub, the home of C&CD consulting limited; promoters of Ignite Megastars, on Wednesday the 2nd of June, 2021, as part of his tour to technology hubs in Lagos, Nigeria. Venia business hub consists of a number of start-ups and tech platforms that cover a wide range of industries. They provide some of the best platforms for new and existing businesses, start-ups and multinationals as well as corporate executives to achieve their business targets. The staff of C&CD Consulting limited where some of the delegates on hand to receive the governor at the hub.
